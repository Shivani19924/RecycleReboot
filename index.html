How it works

  1. 1. Post an item

    Add a short title, category, location and a photo. Mark it free, swap, donate or wanted.

  2. 2. Connect locally

    Chat via your preferred method (share contact info in the description). Meet up safely in public.

  3. 3. Celebrate reuse

    Mark it as picked up. Watch your stats grow as you help the planet.

About Zero-Waste NEO

Zero-Waste NEO is a simple, community-driven platform to reduce waste through gifting and swapping. This version is a clean-room redesign built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS and a distinct UI from other implementations.
